Oatmeal

Description

The best oatmeal I've ever had. No need to top this with sugar afterwards (although it does taste pretty good with sugar). Good with or without milk.

The oatmeal takes 15 minutes total from getting the ingredients to serving and eating. This recipe makes 3 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, whisk together egg, milk, and brown sugar. Mix in the oats afterwards
  2. When oatmeal begins to boil, stir until oatmeal becomes thick. Remove from heat and stir in butter until melted.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy!
