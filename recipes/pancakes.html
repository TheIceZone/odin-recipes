Pancakes

Description

Easy and simple to make pancakes that taste amazing! You can add fruit or other ingredients to the batter before cooking the pancakes to make them taste better.

The pancakes take around 20 minutes total from grabbing the ingreidents to eating them and yeilds 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs (3 medium eggs)
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs untill fluffy; then beat in the sugar and salt. In a seperate bowl, sitr flout and baking powder togehter.
  2. Stir milk and flour mixture alternately into the eggs, starting and ending with milk, until everything is combinded.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium high heat and pout ~1/4 cup of batter on to the griddle at a time. Flip pancakes when tiny holrs appear over the uncooked side.
  4. Cook both sides until brown, then remove pancake, add more oil if needed, and add another 1/4 cup of batter until you have eough pancakes or you run out of batter.
  5. Top with butter, syrup, and/or fresh fruits and enjoy!
